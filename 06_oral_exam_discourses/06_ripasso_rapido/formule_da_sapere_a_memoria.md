# Formule da sapere a memoria

## Green

$$
\oint_C P\,dx+Q\,dy=\iint_D(Q_x-P_y)\,dx\,dy
$$

## Stokes

$$
\oint_C F\cdot\tau\,d\ell=\iint_S\operatorname{rot}(F)\cdot n\,dS
$$

## Gauss

$$
\iint_{\partial\Omega}F\cdot n\,dS=\iiint_\Omega\operatorname{div}F\,dV
$$

## Lagrange

$$
\nabla f=\lambda\nabla g
$$

$$
\nabla f=\lambda\nabla g_1+\mu\nabla g_2
$$

## Integrali linea

$$
\int_C f\,d\ell=\int_a^b f(r(t))\|r'(t)\|\,dt
$$

$$
\int_C F\cdot\tau\,d\ell=\int_a^bF(r(t))\cdot r'(t)\,dt
$$

## Superfici

$$
dS=\|r_u\times r_v\|\,du\,dv
$$

$$
\iint_SF\cdot n\,dS
=
\pm\iint_DF(r(u,v))\cdot(r_u\times r_v)\,du\,dv
$$

## Rotore e divergenza

$$
\operatorname{rot}(F)=
\left(F_{3y}-F_{2z},F_{1z}-F_{3x},F_{2x}-F_{1y}\right)
$$

$$
\operatorname{div}F=F_{1x}+F_{2y}+F_{3z}
$$

## Jacobiani

- Polari: $\rho$.
- Cilindriche: $\rho$.
- Sferiche del corso: $r^2\cos\theta$.
