## Formule

$$
x=\rho\cos\theta,
\qquad
y=\rho\sin\theta.
$$

Il jacobiano e':

$$
\frac{D(x,y)}{D(\rho,\theta)}=\rho.
$$

Quindi:

$$
dx\,dy=\rho\,d\rho\,d\theta.
$$
